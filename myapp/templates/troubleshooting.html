Troubleshooting Center

Here are some common issues and solutions you can try.

Common Issues and Fixes

Issue 1 : Unable to Login

If you're unable to log in, try the following steps:

  • Ensure your username and password are correct.
  • Check if your internet connection is stable.
  • Clear your browser cache and cookies.

Issue 2 : Slow Performance

If the platform is slow, try the following:

  • Clear your browser cache.
  • Ensure you're not running heavy programs in the background.
  • Try using a different browser.
  • Check for any ongoing maintenance or outages on the platform.

Issue 3 : Connectivity Problems

If you're experiencing connectivity issues, try the following:

  • Ensure your internet connection is stable.
  • Try restarting your router.
  • Check for network congestion in your area.
  • Contact your ISP if the problem persists.

Issue 4 : Feature Not Working as Expected

If a feature isn't working as expected, try the following:

  • Check if there are any updates or bug fixes available for the platform.
  • Clear your browser's cache and refresh the page.
  • Re-log into the platform to reset any potential session issues.
  • If the issue persists, contact support for assistance.

Steps to Fix Issues

If you're unable to resolve the issue using the above steps, follow these general troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your internet connection.
  2. Clear your browser cache and cookies.
  3. Restart the application or refresh the page.
  4. Contact support if the problem persists.

If the issue remains unresolved, please contact our support team for further assistance.
